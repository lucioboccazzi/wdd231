Football (Soccer)

Football is the most popular sport in Argentina. The country has a rich football history, with legendary players such as Diego Maradona and Lionel Messi. Argentina has won multiple FIFA World Cups and Copa América titles.

Rugby

Rugby is a widely followed sport in Argentina, with the national team, Los Pumas, being a dominant force in international competitions. Argentina has a deep rugby tradition and a dedicated fan base.

Basketball

Basketball is gaining popularity in Argentina. The national team won the Olympic gold medal in men's basketball in 2004 and continues to produce talented players who compete internationally.

Hockey (Field Hockey)

Field hockey is particularly popular among women in Argentina. The Argentina women's national field hockey team, Las Leonas, is one of the best teams in the world, winning multiple World Cups and Olympic medals.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a well-followed sport in Argentina. Both men's and women's teams perform well internationally, with the men's team achieving success in major competitions like the FIVB World Championship and the Olympics.
